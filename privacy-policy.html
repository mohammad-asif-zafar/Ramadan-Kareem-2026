1. Information We Collect

Location Information

The app may access approximate or precise location to calculate accurate prayer times and display nearby mosques. Location data is used only on your device and is not stored, shared, or sold.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, or payment data.

2. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services to provide core features:

  • Quran text and audio via AlQuran Cloud API
  • Prayer time calculations using the Adhan library by Batoul Apps
  • Maps and nearby mosques powered by Google Maps and Google Places APIs
  • Location services provided by Google Play Services

3. How We Use Information

Information is used strictly to provide app functionality such as prayer times and mosque discovery. The app does not use data for advertising or tracking.

4. Data Storage & Security

The app does not store personal data on external servers. All network communication uses secure HTTPS connections.

5. Permissions

  • Location – Prayer times and nearby mosques
  • Internet – Fetch Quran content and map data
  • Notifications – Prayer reminders
  • Vibration – Notification alerts

6. Children’s Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
